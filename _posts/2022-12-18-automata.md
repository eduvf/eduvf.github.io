**/aŭ.ˈto.ma.ta/** (also written simply as "automata") is a piece for **piano solo** and **electronics**. Two speakers are placed at each side of the piano, from the perspective of the audience, and the pianist uses a MIDI pedal to control a MaxMSP patch that triggers a series of audio samples. _The homogeneous timber between the acoustic and the virtual piano gives a seamless sound that morphs organically._

> Premiered on 2021-01-21 at ESMUC
