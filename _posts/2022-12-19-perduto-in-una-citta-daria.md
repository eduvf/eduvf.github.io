**Perdutto in una Città d'Aria** (_lit. Lost In An Air City_) is a piece for **organ**, specially written for the organ at the _Parròquia de Sant Esteve de Granollers_ within the "**Granoll-Orgue XXI**" project. It takes its title from the Salvatore Sciarrino's piano piece _Perduto in una città d'acque_.

> Premiered on 2022-06-15
