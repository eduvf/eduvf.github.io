**TRUMPet** is a musical experiment using _MaxMSP_, the _fiddle_ library and a _trumpet_. It plays on the words "trumpet" and "Trump" (the former president of the United States), as each phrase played on the instrument triggers a short recording of him.

> Premiered on 2021-06-14
