**Líneas de puntos** (_lit. Dot Lines_) is a piece for **saxophone ensemble** and **live electronics**. It works similar fashion to **MACHINE** and uses the same I/O system with the **Joy-Con controllers** from the Nintendo Switch game console. It was first performed on and written for the "**Kebyart meets ESMUC**" project, together with the **Kebyart saxophone quartet** and many saxophone students from the school.

> Premiered on 2022-05-12 at ESMUC
