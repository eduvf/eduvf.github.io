Hi! I'm **Eduard Vallmitjana Fauró**, a composer/programmer based in Barcelona. Currently, I'm finishing my studies of **music composition** at ESMUC ([Escola Superior de Música de Catalunya](https://www.esmuc.cat/)) and network **systems administration** at IOC ([Institut Obert de Catalunya](https://ioc.xtec.cat/)).

My work usually centers around the **combination of technology and art**, taking the flexibility of sound synthesis, programming and I/O systems, and using it to create new contemporary compositions and performances. This has let me take advantage of my two main areas of knowledge and mix them into projects that involve both the artistic and computing paths.

Through my studies, I've had the opportunity to learn from professional composers, mainly [Aureliano Cattaneo](https://aurelianocattaneo.com/) and [José Río-Pareja](http://www.rio-pareja.com/), and do collaborations with amazing people, like the [Kebyart](https://www.kebyart.com/) saxophone quartet. Many of these projects have been performed and will be available as teasers on this website in the near future!

The tools I use generally change from one project to another, as I try to adapt and explore the best solutions to the problems that arise. However, I do have some experience working with programming languages like _Python_ and _JavaScript_ (I'm also studying _Rust_ and _C_ for some low-level programming), and I've used _[SuperCollider](https://supercollider.github.io/)_ and _[MaxMSP](https://cycling74.com/products/max)_ for more artistic and sound-related projects.
